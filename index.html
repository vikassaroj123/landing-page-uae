Answers to common questions about deploying Zoho for UAE businesses.

We configure VAT, currencies (AED + multi-currency), languages (Arabic / English RTL), payroll components (WPS, EOSB, GPSSA) and integrations needed for UAE operations.

Zoho Books has a UAE-specific edition with built-in 5% VAT, VAT201 returns, reverse charge handling, designated zone treatment, and e-invoicing-ready document workflows aligned with the FTA's upcoming mandate.

Yes. Zoho Payroll UAE generates MOHRE WPS SIF files for salary disbursement, calculates EOSB (gratuity) per UAE Labour Law, and handles GPSSA contributions for UAE/GCC nationals — with Arabic/English payslips delivered through Zoho People self-service.

Zoho integrates with Telr, Network International (N-Genius), PayTabs, Checkout.com, Stripe MENA, and BNPL providers Tabby and Tamara through APIs / Zoho Flow — enabling cards, Apple Pay, and split payments on invoices and Zoho Commerce checkouts.

Zoho supports role-based access, field-level security, encryption, MFA, audit logs and DPA controls — aligned with Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 (PDPL). For Corporate Tax (9% from June 2023), we configure GL, tax-period reporting, and the necessary financial statements in Zoho Books.
